DKBS'25 was front-loaded and had no runway: no tease phase, no waitlist, follow-ups fizzled after day one. Summer Sale '26 proved the fix: daily angle rotation plus a hard close (its two closing sends alone did $7k, 24% of total). This plan adds anticipation on the front and structure in the middle and end.

Assumption: "abc" is a placeholder for the select products the extra 5% stacks on. Confirm SKUs; structure works either way. The extra 5% appears exactly three times: waitlist early access, one mid-sale 24h flash, final call.

1Waitlist (live ~Aug 13, two weeks out)

  • Klaviyo full-page popup, site-wide. New list: DKBS'26 Waitlist.
  • Hook: don't reveal 29%. "Diipa's birthday sale. our biggest of the year. waitlist gets in first, plus a little extra." The extra 5% is the waitlist perk — occasion #1.
  • Popup rules: show once per visitor, suppress on cart/checkout, suppress existing waitlist members, mobile-first.
  • Push existing list to join via 2 emails + 1 SMS. Subscribers who join = self-selected high-intent segment to hit hardest during the sale without burning the full file.

4Standing rules (applied throughout)

  • Active subscribers excluded from all promo sends (no cannibalizing the sub base).
  • SMS: straight apostrophes only, "Inde" not "indē", links auto-shortened. One credit per send where possible.
  • Verification via profile-based previews with real trigger events (no US test number available).

5Flags

Discount stacking. Shopify can't stack two % discounts unless discount combinations is enabled. Cleanest: extra-5% windows run a separate 34% code. Also decide 34% flat vs 5% off the discounted price (~32.6%). Needs a check with Praveen.
Labor Day. Sep 5 is the Saturday of Labor Day weekend (Labor Day is Sep 7). Last year Labor Day sends did $3.6k. Option: quiet 48h encore Sep 6–7 to non-purchasers.
India. Mirror the arc on Klaviyo email + Bitespeed WhatsApp. Birthday/heritage framing lands natively there. Don't copy US copy.
