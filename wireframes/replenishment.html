Implementation notes (for the Klaviyo build)

  • No one-time discount. E1 is a full-price reorder nudge; the only incentive in the flow is the subscription (save [20%]). This protects margin and builds the one-time → subscription motion.
  • Confirm the subscription %. Your Journeys master sheet says 20%; verify against the live Recharge setting before locking copy. Written as a variable [20%] for now.
  • One-tap reorder CTA is the primary mechanic — the current 0.8% click is the image-only design with no live CTA. At minimum, build the CTA as a live button.
  • Timing is consumption-based — set the wait per estimated depletion (e.g. a 90ml node), not a flat 30/37 days. Product vs volume split TBD after the SKU conversation.
  • Copy stays generic (no product name) until the personalization decision — works for either a per-product or a per-volume flow.
  • All gates on Placed Order (exit on reorder); the current flow uses Checkout Started — fix.
  • Coordinate the Delivered-Shipment overload — cross-flow suppression vs Winback / Post-Delivery / Pump.
